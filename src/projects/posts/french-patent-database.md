Creating a comprehensive database of French patents from the Revolution through the Belle Époque, analyzing patterns of innovation during industrialization. This project examines the entire corpus of French patents from 1791 to 1900, providing researchers with detailed information about inventors, locations, and technological categories. The database enables quantitative analysis of innovation trends, the diffusion of technological knowledge, and the role of patents in economic development during this transformative period.
